Add Points to Multiple Entries

Use this feature to award points to participants for special events, bonus challenges, or corrections.

Enter a positive number to add points, or negative to subtract.

Usage Examples:

  • Bonus Event: Add 5 points to all participants for attending a special event
  • Key Stage Bonus: Add 2 points to only KS3 students for participation
  • Correction: Add -3 points to fix an error in scoring
  • Holiday Bonus: Add 1 point to all participants for the holidays

⚠️ Warning

Bulk updates affect multiple entries at once and cannot be undone. Please double-check your settings before proceeding.
